About Us

What is CouGramTion?

Cougramtion is a college recommendation system specifically designed to assist young adults and students in navigating the transition from Senior High School to higher education. Utilizing advanced machine learning algorithms, Cougramtion analyzes students' academic backgrounds, including their Senior High School strand, average grades, and performance in College Entrance Tests, particularly their Overall Ability Percentile Rank.

How it works?

Our state-of-the-art college recommendation system is designed to simplify your search for the perfect college. By leveraging advanced algorithms, it evaluates OAPR(cet), Shs Strand, and Shs Gpa to recommend three colleges that best match for you. Users simply completes the 2 steps to get recommended.
